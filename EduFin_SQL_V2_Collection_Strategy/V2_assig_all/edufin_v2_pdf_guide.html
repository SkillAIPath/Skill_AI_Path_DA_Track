What: We evaluate each customer's ability to pay based on employment type, income level, CIBIL score, and asset indicators to estimate recovery likelihood.

Insight: This helps prioritize legal resources on cases most likely to result in successful recovery, maximizing return on legal investment.
C1-D: Legal Action Timeline
What: We create urgency classifications (IMMEDIATE, URGENT, SCHEDULED) based on days overdue, default amount, and recovery potential to establish legal action priorities.
Why: Legal teams have limited capacity and must prioritize cases. High-value defaults with good recovery potential and sufficient overdue time should get immediate attention.
Insight: This creates a prioritized legal action roadmap showing which cases need immediate filing versus those that can wait for standard processing.
C1-E: Final Combined Emergency Analysis
What: We combine all factors into a comprehensive legal action recommendation report that includes contact verification, documentation completeness, and final case prioritization.
