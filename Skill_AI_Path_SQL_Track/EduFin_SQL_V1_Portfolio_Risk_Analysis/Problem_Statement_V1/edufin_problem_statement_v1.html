üìñ10. Appendix: Definitions & Abbreviations

Loan Status Definitions

  • Active: Loans in current repayment with no missed payments exceeding 30 days
  • Default: Loans with payments overdue by 90+ days or declared uncollectible
  • Closed: Loans fully repaid according to original terms or early settlement
  • Overdue: Loans with payments 30-89 days past due but not yet classified as default

Financial Metrics

  • Exposure: Total outstanding principal balance across loan portfolio
  • Loss: Principal amount written off due to default or collection failure
  • Cohort: Group of loans sharing common characteristics (vintage, institution, geography)
  • Collection Rate: Percentage of scheduled payments successfully collected within reporting period

Institutional Classifications

  • Tier 1: Premier institutions with accreditation, high placement rates, and partnership priority
  • Tier 2: Established institutions with solid performance but standard partnership terms
  • Tier 3: Emerging or specialized institutions requiring enhanced monitoring
  • Partnership Revenue: Total income generated from institutional relationships including referral fees

Risk Assessment Terms

  • Portfolio at Risk: Percentage of total portfolio showing payment stress or default indicators
  • Vintage Analysis: Performance comparison across loan origination time periods
  • Credit Migration: Movement of accounts between risk categories over time
  • Early Warning Signals: Leading indicators predicting potential payment problems
